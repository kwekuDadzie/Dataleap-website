About Us

Innovative Healthcare With Technology & Data

Dataleap Technologies LTD was founded to bridge the technological gap in Ghana's healthcare system. We provide digital solutions that help healthcare facilities optimize operations, improve patient care, and ensure safe medication dispensing. Our expertise spans HealthTech, MedTech, and Digital Health innovations.
